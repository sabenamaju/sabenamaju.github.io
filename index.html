Hasty and Tasty

Extreme mood, energy, and behavioral swings are characteristics of bipolar disorder, a chronic mood illness. The primary symptoms of bipolar disorder are manic and hypomanic episodes, with depressive episodes also occurring in the majority of cases. With the help of medication, talk therapy, lifestyle modifications, and other treatments, the illness is controllable.
